What is #ify?

#ify (spelt "hashify") is a way of finding links that people are talking about. Search engines are good for finding information relating to a string of words that articles have included in them. #ify is for finding articles that people are talking about on the topic. Instead of any website that uses the word "iPhone" in it; we look for what people are linking to with the hashtag "#iPhone". This means you see the articles that people are talking about. #ify gets results from Twitter, Google+ and Facebook.

What can I do to help out?

#ify is currently a free and ad-free service, and we plan to keep it that way. One way you could help out is to register domain names for this website (like http://hashify.org, http://hashify.com, http://hashifysearch.com, etc). If you have a domain you would like to share with us just setup a redirect to this address. It also would be really nice if you could promote the service or contribute to the actual code of the service.
